G-MAN

G-Man Skibidi Toilet is the main antagonist of the short-lived series "Skibidi Toilet". This is the most powerful of the Skibidi toilets, as well as their mighty leader. The G-Toilet increases in size with each episode and has unique abilities for it. His main ability is to release orange laser beams from his eyes, which is shown to be a deadly and destructive attack.
